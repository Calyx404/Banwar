Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 6, 2025

The Banwar Project Team (“we,” “our,” “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through compliance with this Privacy Policy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under applicable laws, including the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and, for international visitors, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Section 1. Information We Collect
  • Geolocation Data (Optional): With your browser's permission, we may collect your approximate location to improve map performance and display relevant geographic data. This information is processed temporarily and never stored on our servers.
  • Technical Logs: Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages / Render) may collect limited information such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps for security, abuse prevention, and performance monitoring. See the GitHub Privacy Statement ↗ .
  • Voluntary Communications: If you contact us via email or other channels outside the website, we will use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry and handle it in accordance with applicable laws.
Section 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information only to:


  • Improve the map's user experience: We may use your browser's geolocation data to adjust the initial map view so that it loads more efficiently and displays relevant geographic areas. This helps reduce map loading time, enhances accuracy, and provides a smoother interaction, especially for users accessing the site from within the Philippines or nearby regions.
  • Maintain site security and reliability: Technical logs automatically generated by our hosting providers are used to detect malicious activities, monitor uptime, and resolve performance issues. This ensures the website remains stable, secure from abuse, and available to legitimate users, aligning with best practices for infrastructure monitoring.
  • Comply with legal obligations: Certain minimal technical data may be retained temporarily as required by law, or to respond to lawful requests. This enables us to meet applicable legal, security, and academic archival obligations under the Philippine Data Privacy Act and relevant international frameworks.

We do not sell or share data with advertisers or third parties.

Section 3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We do not use cookies or local storage for tracking or analytics. Your browser or CDN caches may temporarily store static assets for performance. GitHub Pages or Render may set technical cookies on their own domains; these are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Section 4. Your Rights

Under RA 10173 and GDPR, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data processed through this site. Since we collect minimal data, these rights will typically apply only if you voluntarily contacted us. To exercise your rights, email us at: banwar.connect@gmail.com ↗ .

Section 5. Data Security and Retention

We use HTTPS and rely on secure hosting providers. We do not retain geolocation data. Technical logs are managed by hosting providers and are not accessible for profiling or analytics.

Section 6. Children's Privacy

The site is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Section 7. Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in legal requirements or site practices. Revisions will be posted with a new effective date.
